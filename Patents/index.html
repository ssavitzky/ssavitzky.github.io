I'm actually pretty strongly opposed to software patents -- they apparently exist for no other purpose than to be abused. However, I worked at an R&D lab, and patents are one their main products. This list would give you a pretty good idea of what I worked on there, except that they're all written in deliberately obfuscatory legalese.

(By the way, if you just search for IN/savitzky, many of the hits you'll get will belong to my father.)
